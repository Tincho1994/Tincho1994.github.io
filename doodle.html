Doodle: A Robotic drawing Companion

Summer - Fall 2017
Personal Project/ Master's of Engineering Project
Ithaca, NY

The goal of this project is to create a robot that would be able to observe a childâ€™s drawing and draw alongside them. The vision is for this robot to enhance drawing as a recreational and creative outlet for children through human-robot cooperation.

The basis for the vision system was built using openCV. The current proof of concept model detects the canvas space of a piece of paper, transforms the image to a top down view of the drawing, and then transforms the detected contours of the drawing into G-Code.
