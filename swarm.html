Swarm Flocking with Humans Expirement

Fall 2017
Human Robot Interaction Final Project
Ithaca, NY

The purpose of this final project was to implement a robotic system to explore a concept of human robot interaction and conduct an expirement. For our project we chose to examine human perceptions of swarms in a following scenario. To conduct our expirement we designed a following algorithm that would allow multiple autonomous robots to follow a human at a given distance while mitigating collisions. The system is composed of sphero robots communicating via bluetooth to a central computer and a vicon tracking system that collects the location of sphero robots and human participants.

The algorithm we designed makes use of velocity matching for following a human as well as a social forces model to repel robots from each other and maintain a desired deistance form the human. This algortihm is descirbed in greater detail as well as with our expirement in this short paper we wrote.
